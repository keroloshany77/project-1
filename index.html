What is the UEFA Champions League

How many teams in the tournament

The UEFA Champions League tournament of 32 teams that compete in five rounds

Champions League system

The tournament is being conducted Starting from the round of 16, as is the current system, while the teams ranked 24th to 36th will be completely eliminated from the tournament

The road to the playoffs

The top teams in each group will qualify for the round of 16, while the teams ranked 9th to 24th will compete in a two-legged playoff, and teams ranked 25th or lower will be eliminated.

How long is the tournament

The tournament lasts for 12 months. The tournament begins on July 10 and ends on May 31

The team that won the tournament the most

The team that has won the most Champions League titles is "Real Madrid"

The player who won the tournament the most

“Toni Kroos, Nacho Fernandez, Luka Modric, and Daniel Carvajal” are the most decorated players in the tournament.

The all-time top scorer in the Champions League

The all-time top scorer in the Champions League is Portuguese star Cristiano Ronaldo

Ronaldo holds the record for the most goals in the history of the Champions League, having scored more than 140 goals. These goals came through his participation in more than 180 matches in the tournament

Champions League history

Since its launch in 1955, it has been the European Champions League One of the most prominent football tournaments in the world, where the strongest European clubs compete to achieve glory and win the most expensive title

The importance of champions league in the world

The Champions League is one of the most popular tournaments In which the competition is very strong, and every match is important and decisive, and this increases the excitement, attractiveness and fun of the tournament

Some of the achievements of the most famous players who participated in the tournament

Lionel Messi

Messi is one of the top scorers in the Champions League in history, as he scored more than 120 goals and was the top scorer of the tournament in 6 different seasons He won the championship title four times with Barcelona He won the Champions League Player of the Year award several times

Luka Modric

Modric won the UEFA European Footballer of the Year and UEFA World Player of the Year awards in 2018and has won the tournament five times with Real Madrid. Modric gave a terrifying performance against the strongest European teams

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah, the Egyptian king who broke many records in the Champions League, won the championship title once with Liverpool and was the fastest player to score 50 goals with Liverpool in this tournament. He was also nominated for the European Player of the Year award
